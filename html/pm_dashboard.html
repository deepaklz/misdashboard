ℹ️ Story Points Convention
🎯 1 Issue = 0.6 SP = 6 Hours
Each issue = 0.6 SP = 6 hours of work. A 0.2 SP task = 2 hours. SP is proportional: every 0.1 SP = 1 hour of effort.
✅ Committed SP = Hours Planned
Committed SP = issues × 0.6. Multiply by 10 to get planned hours. E.g. 3.0 SP = 30 hours planned for the sprint.
📦 Delivered SP = Hours Completed
Delivered SP = Done issues × 0.6. Multiply by 10 for completed hours. Delivery rate = Delivered ÷ Committed × 100%.
